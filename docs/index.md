# U.S. Marine Energy Resource

## Coming Soon

Full documentation for the U.S. Marine Energy Resource characterization datasets is on its way.

In the meantime, you can explore the data directly:

- [Marine Energy Atlas](https://maps.nlr.gov/marine-energy-atlas) — interactive map of U.S. marine energy resources
- [MHKDR Tidal Dataset](https://mhkdr.openei.org/submissions/632) — download tidal hindcast data
- [MHKDR Wave Dataset](https://mhkdr.openei.org/submissions/326) — download wave hindcast data

**Questions?** Email [marineresource@nlr.gov](mailto:marineresource@nlr.gov)
